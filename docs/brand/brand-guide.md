# Brand Guide

Aredir Labs company brand direction for aredirlabs.com and aligned product marketing.

## Brand essence

**Aredir Labs** builds focused software products for sports, fitness, and operations teams. The company site should communicate clarity, competence, and craft—not hype.

## Visual direction (placeholder)

> Refine with final logo and palette in `logo-direction.md` before major visual implementation.

- **Tone:** Clean, modern, professional
- **Palette:** TBD — document primary, secondary, neutral, and semantic colors when finalized
- **Typography:** TBD — pair a readable sans for UI and body; optional display face for hero only

## Logo usage

- Minimum clear space around the mark
- Do not stretch, rotate, or apply unapproved effects
- See [logo direction](./logo-direction.md)

## Photography and illustration

- Prefer authentic, purposeful imagery over generic stock
- Illustrations should match line weight and color system when introduced

## Application on web

- Consistent header/footer branding
- Favicon and social share image (`og:image`) per deployment checklist
- Accessible color contrast (WCAG AA minimum for text)
